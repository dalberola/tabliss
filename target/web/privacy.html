Privacy Policy

Service: Tabliss at diginaut.es · Version 2026-06-25 · Last updated 25 June 2026

This policy explains what personal data Stackly (stackly.ch), Geneva, Switzerland ("we") processes when you use the Tabliss web app at diginaut.es and the optional account/sync service at auth.diginaut.es. We process personal data under Swiss data-protection law (nFADP) and, where applicable, the EU GDPR.

Using Tabliss without an account

Tabliss works fully offline in your browser. Your dashboard configuration (widgets, layout, theme, language, background choices) and any background images you upload are stored locally in your browser and are never sent to us unless you choose to create an account and enable sync.

Account & preference sync (optional)

If you create an account to sync your preferences, we process:

Uploaded background images stay in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers.

Cookies

When logged in, the account service sets one strictly necessary cookie — a refresh_token (HttpOnly, Secure, scoped to /auth) that keeps your session active. It is not used for tracking or advertising.

Analytics & error reporting (third parties)

The Tabliss web app loads the following third-party services:

  • Matomo (self-hosted analytics, loaded from go.stackly.ch) — configured with IP anonymisation to measure aggregate usage.
  • Sentry (sentry.io) — collects crash and error reports (including technical details such as browser and error context) to help us fix bugs.

Hosting & email processors

The website, account API, database, and verification/password-reset email are hosted and sent by Infomaniak (Switzerland), acting as our processor.

Data retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Account Sync widget ("Delete account"), which removes your account and synced settings. We also automatically delete accounts after 12 months of inactivity (no sign-in or sync); before deletion we email a reminder so you can keep the account by signing in. Email-verification and password-reset tokens are short-lived and deleted automatically when they expire.

Your rights

Under Swiss data-protection law (nFADP) and, where applicable, the GDPR, you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, and portability of your personal data, and object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@diginaut.es. You may also lodge a complaint with the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) or, in the EU, your local supervisory authority.

Changes

We may update this policy; the version date above will change. Material changes affecting account holders may require re-acceptance.
